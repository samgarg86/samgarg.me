I'm a Web/Mobile Creative Front-End Developer from Sydney, Australia with 8 years of online experience. I have extensive experience in building small to large scale cross-device customer facing websites. I specialise in Responsive and Fluid web solutions, allowing businesses to create and maintain only 1 website for desktop, tablet and mobile. I love building User Interfaces with a focus on minimalistic design, and I worship that godly person who once upon a time, over the course of a few too many beers uttered the holy words 'less is more'! I have also created many successful Web Promotions and Online Ad Campaigns.

I mainly focus on on HTML5, CSS3, Javascript and jQuery development.

In June 2013, I moved to Amsterdam, Netherlands and worked for several companies in Amsterdam including DigitasLbi LostBoys, one of the top Creative Agencies in Amsterdam, where I built several Responsive-Web E-Commerce solutions for clients including Scotch & Soda, ScS and VERTU. Prior to that I took on a job at Funda, the leading residential rental website in the Netherlands. Before moving to Amsterdam, I spent 3 years at Ninemsn, Sydney, a leading Online Media company in Australia, jointly owned by Microsoft and Channel 9. I worked in various teams in Ninemsn leading, developing, and supporting their entire suite of 80+ websites.

I have also been actively exploring my entrepreneurial side and co-founded a few startups including a travel video sharing website which was successfully launched in Australia in 2011.

When I'm not coding, I'm either travelling or taking pictures, or both. In the last 4 years I've visited 20 countries across Asia and Europe. This has exposed me to a lot of new people and cultures, which is an asset in today's multi-cultural team environments. I like going to islands and capture nature, beaches and scenery. Lately I've been doing a lot of portraits, events and street photography. I love to shoot in 35mm! You can see some of my work on flickr. Oh, I'm also trying to learn Spanish on the side, but I still suck at it :(
