About

I'm a Web/Mobile Creative Front-End Developer from Sydney, Australia with 8 years of online experience. I have extensive experience in building small to large scale cross-device customer facing web portals. I specialise in Responsive and Fluid web solutions, allowing businesses to create and maintain only 1 website for desktop, tablet and mobile. I also have a lot of experience building Web Promotions and online Ad Campaigns. I mainly focus on on HTML5, CSS3, Javascript and jQuery development.

In June 2013, I moved to Amsterdam, Netherlands in search for a new life, and I now call Amsterdam home. I've worked for several companies in Amsterdam including DigitasLbi LostBoys, one of the top Creative Agencies in Amsterdam, where I built several Responsive-Web E-Commerce solutions for clients including Scotch & Soda, ScS and VERTU. Prior to that I worked for Funda, the leading residential rental website in the Netherlands. Before moving to Amsterdam, I worked at Ninemsn, Sydney for 3 years, a leading Online Media company in Australia, jointly owned by Microsoft and Channel 9. I have worked in various teams in Ninemsn leading, developing, and supporting their entire suite of 80+ websites.

I have also been actively exploring my entrepreneurial side and co-founded a few startups including a travel video sharing website which was successfully launched in Australia in 2011.

On a personal front, I'm an avid traveller and in the last 4 years I've visited 20 countries across Asia and Europe. This has exposed me to a lot of new people and cultures, which is an asset in today's multi-cultural team environments.
