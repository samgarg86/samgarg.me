Sam Garg is a front-end web developer from Sydney, Australia. He's been building pixel-perfect consumer facing websites and e-commerce online shopfronts for over 10 years. Specialises in responsive web solutions, allowing businesses to create and maintain only one website for desktop, tablet and mobile. Sam also has an eye for design, loves designing websites himself and working with designers, building great user-experiences together.

Sam's main focus is HTML5, CSS3, Javascript and jQuery, very passionate about the latest web technologies and writes good well-organised code.

A Software Engineering graduate from University of Technology Sydney, Sam Garg has worked for several big names in Sydney and Amsterdam doing web development, building e-commerce shopfronts, creating online promotions and ad-campaigns, and even leading small scrum teams/projects. Now Sam works as a remote web freelancer, helping mainly startups and small businesses improve their online presence.

In his free time Sam is either travelling, chilling at a beach or taking pictures. Been to over 30 countries around Asia, Europe and Latin America and not done yet!
